<br />
Big group dinner, one person paid, and now they&#39;re Venmo-chasing half the table for two weeks. And somehow the person who got the steak and two cocktails is paying the same as the person who had a salad.<br />
<br />
A fellow Haas student built TidyTab (<a data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?q=https://haas.campusgroups.com/click?email_links_id%3D398954%26member_id%3D26193431&amp;source=gmail&amp;ust=1778716679447000&amp;usg=AOvVaw0xVgsBaGPb26z91agNGnfC" href="https://haas.campusgroups.com/click?email_links_id=398994&member_id=26205835" style="color:#1155cc" target="_blank">tidytab.app</a>) to make that part less miserable. You scan the receipt and share a link. Each person contributes by choosing their dish (or even-split), and the group can see the running total.<br />
<br />
<u>Works well for:</u><br />
- Big cohort events<br />
- Birthday dinners<br />
- Any group spend where people ate (or drank) very differently<br />

<p style="text-align:left">Going to SF or the airport soon? Uber too expensive?<br />
<br />
A Haas student built <b>Poolsy</b>, a simple way to match with another Berkeley student going the same way at a similar time and split the cost.<br />
<br />
- Save up to 50% on your ride<br />
- Match with verified students only<br />
- Exact pickup and dropoff<br />
<br />
<u>How it works:</u><br />
1. Post your ride in about 30 seconds<br />
2. Get matched with someone going your way<br />
3. Coordinate and book the ride together<br />
<br />
