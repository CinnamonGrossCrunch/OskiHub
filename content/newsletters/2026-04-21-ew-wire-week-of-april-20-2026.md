<p style="text-align: left;">Going to SF or the airport soon? Uber too expensive?<br />
<br />

<br />
- Save up to 50% on your ride<br />
- Match with verified students only<br />
- Exact pickup and dropoff<br />
<br />
<u>How it works:</u><br />
1. Post your ride in about 30 seconds<br />
2. Get matched with someone going your way<br />
3. Coordinate and book the ride together<br />
