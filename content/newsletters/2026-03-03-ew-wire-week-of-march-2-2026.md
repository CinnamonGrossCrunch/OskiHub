<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size:16px;"><strong>The Financial Women of San Francisco (FWSF) Scholarship Fund</strong></span></p>

<p style="text-align: left;">The&nbsp;<a data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?q=https://haas.campusgroups.com/click?email_links_id%3D398287%26member_id%3D26193431&amp;source=gmail&amp;ust=1772574138610000&amp;usg=AOvVaw3_X7_d4qODJDt1R6fkU7Md" href="https://haas.campusgroups.com/click?email_links_id=398334&member_id=26205835" target="_blank">Financial Women of San Francisco</a>&nbsp;(FWSF) Scholarship Fund provides graduate and undergraduate scholarships to San Francisco Bay Area women pursuing careers in the fields of accounting, finance, and/or the financial services industry. Since 1985, the Scholarship Fund has awarded $3.5 million in scholarship grants to Bay Area women. Beyond financial support, FWSF scholarship recipients have the opportunity to be mentored by FWSF members and attend career development and networking events. For more information on who is eligible and how to apply check out this&nbsp;<a data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?q=https://haas.campusgroups.com/click?email_links_id%3D398288%26member_id%3D26193431&amp;source=gmail&amp;ust=1772574138610000&amp;usg=AOvVaw3g-dDJrbqDdYB-b0SOwEsh" href="https://haas.campusgroups.com/click?email_links_id=398335&member_id=26205835" target="_blank">flyer</a>.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
